# PumpkinBiomes+

## Install
- Put `PumpkinBiomesPlus-1.0.jar` in your server `plugins/` folder.
- Restart the server.

## Usage
PumpkinBiomes+ adds biome-based effects that apply to players depending on their location.

Effects include buffs, debuffs, environmental hazards, and visual effects like fog or particles.

Each biome has unique behavior, encouraging exploration and strategy.

## Commands & permissions
- `/biome info` — `pumpkin.biomes.use`
- `/biome reload` — `pumpkin.biomes.admin`
