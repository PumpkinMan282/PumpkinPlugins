# CustomEnchantments+

## Install
- Put `PumpkinEnchants+.jar` in your server `plugins/` folder.
- Restart the server.

## Usage
Custom enchants can be applied to items and provide enhanced effects beyond vanilla Minecraft. Enchants activate based on triggers such as attacking, mining, or taking damage.

Multiple enchants can be combined to create synergy effects, unlocking stronger abilities when specific combinations are present.

Enchant levels scale their strength, and certain enchants may conflict to prevent overpowered combinations.

## Commands & permissions
- `/enchant give <player> <enchant> <level>` — `pumpkin.enchants.admin`
- `/enchant remove <player> <enchant>` — `pumpkin.enchants.admin`
- `/enchant list` — `pumpkin.enchants.use`
